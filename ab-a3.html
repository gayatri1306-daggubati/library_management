Blood Relations

Theory Explanation:

Blood relation reasoning deals with solving problems based on family relationships. These problems require logical interpretation of relationships like father, mother, brother, sister, uncle, aunt, cousin, etc.

Basic Relationships Table:

Relationship Male Female
Father/Mother Father Mother
Sibling Brother Sister
Uncle/Aunt Uncle Aunt
Grandparent Grandfather Grandmother
Child Son Daughter

Types of Blood Relation Questions:

  • Direct Relations: Straightforward relationships like father, mother, brother, sister, etc.
  • Coded Relations: Relationships represented using symbols (e.g., A + B means A is the father of B).
  • Puzzle-based Blood Relations: A complex scenario requiring logical interpretation.

Key Techniques to Solve Blood Relation Problems:

  • Use a family tree diagram to visualize relationships.
  • Start with the known person and work step by step.
  • Break complex statements into smaller parts.
  • Use gender-specific clues to eliminate wrong options.

MCQs on Blood Relations

1. Pointing to a man, Rohan said, "He is the son of my grandfather’s only son." How is the man related to Rohan?

a) Brother
b) Father
c) Uncle
d) Cousin

Answer: a) Brother

2. A man said to a woman, "Your mother’s husband’s sister is my aunt." How is the woman related to the man?

a) Sister
b) Cousin
c) Daughter
d) Niece

Answer: b) Cousin

3. If A is the father of B and C is the brother of A, how is C related to B?

a) Father
b) Uncle
c) Grandfather
d) Cousin

Answer: b) Uncle

4. Pointing to a girl, Aman said, "She is the daughter of my sister's only brother." How is the girl related to Aman?

a) Niece
b) Daughter
c) Cousin
d) Sister

Answer: b) Daughter

5. Ravi is the brother of Seema. Seema is the daughter of Anjali. How is Ravi related to Anjali?

a) Brother
b) Son
c) Father
d) Uncle

Answer: b) Son

6. If P is the mother of Q and Q is the father of R, how is P related to R?

a) Grandmother
b) Mother
c) Aunt
d) Cousin

Answer: a) Grandmother

7. Raj said, "The only son of my father is my daughter's father." How is Raj related to his daughter?

a) Grandfather
b) Uncle
c) Father
d) Brother

Answer: c) Father

8. Rahul is the son of Aman’s father’s sister. Sahil is the son of Divya, who is the mother of Aman. How is Rahul related to Sahil?

a) Brother
b) Cousin
c) Uncle
d) Nephew

Answer: b) Cousin

9. Introducing a woman, Rahul said, "She is the only daughter of my wife’s father." How is the woman related to Rahul?

a) Mother
b) Wife
c) Aunt
d) Sister

Answer: b) Wife

10. If A is the father of B and B is the mother of C, what is the relationship between A and C?

a) Uncle
b) Grandfather
c) Son
d) Brother

Answer: b) Grandfather
