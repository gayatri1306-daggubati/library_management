Syllogism

Theory Explanation:

Syllogism is a type of logical reasoning that consists of statements (premises) leading to a conclusion.

Types of Statements:

  • Universal Affirmative (A type): All A are B.
  • Universal Negative (E type): No A is B.
  • Particular Affirmative (I type): Some A are B.
  • Particular Negative (O type): Some A are not B.

Basic Rules of Syllogism:

  • Two universal affirmative statements lead to a universal affirmative conclusion.
  • One universal affirmative + one universal negative leads to a universal negative conclusion.
  • Two particular statements do not lead to a definite conclusion.
  • If one statement is particular, the conclusion must be particular.
  • Negative conclusions can only be drawn from at least one negative statement.

MCQs on Syllogism

1. Statements:

All apples are fruits.
All fruits are healthy.

Conclusion:

a) All apples are healthy.
b) Some fruits are apples.
c) Both a and b.
d) None of these.

Answer: c) Both a and b.

2. Statements:

No bird is a cat.
All cats are animals.

Conclusion:

a) No bird is an animal.
b) Some animals are not birds.
c) Both a and b.
d) None of these.

Answer: d) None of these.

3. Statements:

Some pens are pencils.
All pencils are erasers.

Conclusion:

a) Some pens are erasers.
b) All erasers are pencils.
c) Some pencils are pens.
d) None of these.

Answer: d) None of these.

4. Statements:

Some books are novels.
No novel is a magazine.

Conclusion:

a) Some books are not magazines.
b) Some magazines are not books.
c) No book is a magazine.
d) None of these.

Answer: d) None of these.

5. Statements:

All roses are flowers.
Some flowers are red.

Conclusion:

a) Some roses are red.
b) All flowers are roses.
c) Both a and b.
d) None of these.

Answer: d) None of these.

6. Statements:

Some men are doctors.
All doctors are intelligent.

Conclusion:

a) Some men are intelligent.
b) All intelligent people are doctors.
c) Both a and b.
d) None of these.

Answer: a) Some men are intelligent.

7. Statements:

All engineers are professionals.
Some professionals are teachers.

Conclusion:

a) Some teachers are engineers.
b) Some professionals are engineers.
c) Both a and b.
d) None of these.

Answer: b) Some professionals are engineers.
